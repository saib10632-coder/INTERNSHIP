# Problem Statement

Manivtha Tours & Travels is a car rental and transportation company in Hyderabad serving outstation trips, airport transfers, corporate contracts, and long-distance tours. The company offers prepaid travel wallets where customers load credit and use it across multiple bookings with automatic deduction — but this process is currently handled manually through calls, WhatsApp messages, spreadsheets, and paper registers.

Without a dedicated digital system, wallet balances are tracked inconsistently, transaction histories are scattered, auto-deductions are missed or applied incorrectly, and management cannot generate real-time reports. Customer interaction history is not maintained centrally, leading to poor follow-up and lost revenue opportunities.

The Customer Prepaid Travel Wallet System solves this by providing a centralised platform for wallet creation, balance tracking, auto-deduction management, transaction ledger processing, automated alerts, and analytics — increasing advance cash flow and locking in repeat customers.
